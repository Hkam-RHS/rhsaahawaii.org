February 22nd, 2019.

RHS Class of 1963 Luncheon

February 22nd, 2019. 1130 to 2pm.

What better way to kick off 2019? We've locked in the date for our next get together and are sending this invitation out early so that you can write in February 22, 2019 on next year's calendar today. Yes, you'll notice that we will be partying at a different place this time as The Willows will be closing at the end of this year for renovation work. We look forward to seeing Naomi Kunihiro Moore who is making the trek from France to join us at this special gathering of friends. As Hale Ikena is on military property, you will have to go through clearance protocol (driver's license, vehicle safety check and registration) before heading up to the restaurant. The buffet is a reasonable $18, tip inclusive. We're anticipating seeing even more of you this time around, so call/email Darrell Mattos or Joy Miyashiro and sign up for an entertaining day ... no planned program, just spontaneous fun. Bring another classmate and we'll save a seat for you!

  • Darrell Mattos. jadeproperties@hawaii.rr.com. 225-3584
  • Joy Miyashiro. joy_miyashiro@hotmail.com. 255-5964
