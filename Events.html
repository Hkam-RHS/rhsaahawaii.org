Sunday, March 26, 2018

2nd Annual Rough Rider Roundup

A golf tournament to benefit Roosevelt High School Athletics

  • 10:30am Check-in
  • $150/Lunch/Banquet

Download PDF for more info.


How your donation helps

The money raised from The 2nd Annual Rough Rider Roundup goes directly to the Roosevelt Alumni Foundation. The RAF then provides money straight to the Roosevelt Football and Golf teams. By joining us for a fun day of golf, Roosevelt Football and Golf programs get additional funds, outside of ther normal budget, to cover costs as they see fit. This is how you have helped Roosevelt High School. Because of you, more students are achieving their dreams by participating in sports. A big mahalo for your kokua and hoping you consider participating in the 3rd Annual Rough Rider Roundup next year!


Event Includes:

  • 18 holes of golf
  • Golf cart
  • Range balls
  • Bento lunch and beverages
  • Pre-tournament putting contest
  • Closest to the pin contests
  • Longest drive contest
  • Hole in one contest
  • Door prizes
  • Buffet dinner at Leilehua Clubhouse
  • Entertainment

Price:

  • $150. Individual entrant
  • $450. Three-man team.

  • $30. Buffet dinner only
